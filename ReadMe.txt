Multi_Threaded3 detects cpu thread_count and will assign tasks accordingly. Be careful with lower-end CPU coolers or overclocked settings, it can stress your CPU extremely.
If you experience extreme high temperatures try to lower your voltages manually. GoodLuck!



Settings in benchmark.json:

bench_mode=1 - Threadpool cpu allocation
bench_mode=2 - older thread cpu allocation


Settings in settings.json:

cpu_load=1 - math algorithm1 stress test

cpu_load=2 - math algorithm2 sterss test
